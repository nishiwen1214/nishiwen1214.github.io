Shiwen Ni (倪仕文)

I am now an Assistant Professor/Postdoc in Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Cheng Kung University.

My research interest includes (but is not limited to) Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.

For a full list of publications and citation counts, visit my Google Scholar page.

You can contact me via email: sw.ni@siat.ac.cn.
